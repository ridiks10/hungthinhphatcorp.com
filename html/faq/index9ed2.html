Answers to questions

The company STORTI INVESTMENTS LTD works in the area of highly liquid real estate in resort zones around the world. By working with us you can always be sure of the success of your investments.

Look at our domain registration. We registered it at the very start of our endeavor.

Tell me more about your company
During our time we've acquired vast experience in real estate investment. We specialize in resort zone real estate all around the world since this segment is less exposed to problems in the world economy. In the section "Our projects" You can become familiar with projects in which we've participated and are participating. For the most part this is construction of residential complexes and commercial real estate
+
How do you receive this income?
There are numerous ways. One of our company's departments, for example, deals with new building construction. During construction the developer provides the lowest prices for the construction of homes and apartments, and in a year or two after construction these objects become more valuable and are quickly sold at a significantly higher price than that for which they were built.
+
Are you interested in small deposits?
Yes, of course, with small deposits we buy inexpensive objects and then immediately rent them out in the long term, or we resell them at a hight price. You can invest as little as 10$
+
How can I replenish my balance in my personal account?
At the moment we accept Perfect Money, AdvCash, Payeer, Bitcoin. This list is convenient for 95% of investors. But if you have any problems - write us and we'll find a suitable way for you to replenish your account.
+
How are my earnings credited?
Percentages are credited every cycle, Funds are withdrawn instantly by manual request. In case of any technical problems, there may be a delay of up to 12 hours. In the near future we plan on doing automatic withdrawals of earnings without your participation
+
Can I avoid withdrawing funds and create a new deposit with them?
Yes, of course

How many deposits can I make?
As many as you want. There are no limitations

Yes. We have an affiliate program with which you can earn 10% of an attracted investor's deposit.
